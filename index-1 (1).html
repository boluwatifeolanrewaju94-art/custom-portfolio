Our Story

Born from Passion.
Brewed with Purpose.

Luminal Brew began in a small Lagos kitchen in 2018, where our founder Adaeze Okafor discovered that the right coffee could transform not just a morning — but a life. Today, we source from 12+ countries, working directly with farmers to bring you coffee that tells a story.

We believe in transparency, sustainability, and the relentless pursuit of the perfect cup. Every bag is roasted to order, ensuring maximum freshness reaches your doorstep.

How We Do It

From Farm to Your Cup

01
Source & Select

We travel to origin — Ethiopia, Colombia, Yemen, Indonesia — and hand-pick the finest specialty lots from trusted farmers.

02
Roast to Profile

Each origin gets a custom roast profile developed over weeks of cupping, designed to maximise its unique character.

Meet the Founder

Adaeze Okafor

Founder & Head Roaster

Adaeze grew up in Enugu, Nigeria, where morning coffee was a sacred family ritual. After studying Food Science at the University of Lagos and spending three years training under master roasters in Denmark and Ethiopia, she returned home with a singular mission: to bring world-class specialty coffee to Africa.

In 2018, she launched Luminal Brew from a 10-square-metre kitchen in Lagos. Six years later, Luminal Brew serves over 48,000 customers across Nigeria, Ghana, and Kenya — and was named one of Africa's Top 10 Emerging Coffee Brands in 2023.
